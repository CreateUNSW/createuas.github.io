What We Do

CREATE runs and provides:
  • - Workshops for beginners on electronics, programming, soldering, prototyping and 3D modelling.
  • - Weekly sales of electronic components on-campus, including Kits
  • - Hackerspaces and makerspaces
  • - Quadcopters
  • - Funding and support for student projects
